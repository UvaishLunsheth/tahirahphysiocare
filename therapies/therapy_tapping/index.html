What is Kinesio Taping?

Kinesio Taping is a modern therapeutic technique used by physiotherapists and sports professionals to support muscles and joints without restricting natural movement. Unlike rigid sports tape, Kinesio Tape is elastic, breathable and mimics the skin‚Äôs flexibility.
