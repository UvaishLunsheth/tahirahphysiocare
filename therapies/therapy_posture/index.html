What is Posture Correction Therapy?

Posture Correction Therapy focuses on identifying muscle imbalance, joint stiffness, alignment issues and movement habits that lead to long-term back, neck and shoulder pain. Physiotherapists use strengthening, mobility training, ergonomic correction and activation therapy to restore natural posture.

Signs You Need Posture Correction

  • Forward head posture
  • Rounded shoulders
  • Slouched mid-back (kyphotic posture)
  • Lower back stiffness or excessive arch
  • Neck, shoulder or upper back pain
  • Uneven shoulders or tilted pelvis
  • Postural fatigue while sitting or standing

Benefits of Posture Correction Therapy

  • Reduces back, neck and shoulder pain
  • Improves breathing and rib mobility
  • Aligns spine and shoulder position
  • Strengthens weak postural muscles
  • Enhances flexibility and movement awareness
  • Prevents chronic posture-related injuries
  • Improves confidence and overall body mechanics

Techniques We Use for Posture Correction

Our treatment combines hands-on therapy, targeted exercises and ergonomic education:

  • Postural muscle strengthening
  • Scapular stability training
  • Core activation exercises
  • Myofascial release for tight muscles
  • Manual correction & mobilizations
  • Stretching of shortened muscle groups
  • Ergonomic & workstation guidance
  • Movement re-training

Who Can Benefit?

  • Students & office workers
  • People with long sitting hours
  • Mobile/Computer workstation users
  • Patients with chronic back or neck pain
  • Individuals with scoliosis or spinal deviations
  • Anyone wanting long-term posture improvement
