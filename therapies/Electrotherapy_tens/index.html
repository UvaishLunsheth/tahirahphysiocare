TENS Electrotherapy

TENS (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation) is a safe, non-invasive electrical therapy used to relieve pain, relax muscles and improve nerve response.

Book TENS Session

What is TENS Electrotherapy?

TENS therapy uses low-voltage electrical impulses delivered through the skin to reduce pain signals and stimulate natural healing. It is widely used for both acute and chronic pain conditions.

Conditions Treated with TENS Therapy

  • Back and lower back pain
  • Neck pain & stiffness
  • Joint pain (knee, shoulder, wrist, ankle)
  • Sciatica and nerve pain
  • Frozen shoulder
  • Post-operative pain
  • Arthritis & degenerative joint pain
  • Muscle spasms

Benefits of TENS Electrotherapy

  • Instant pain relief during and after treatment
  • Reduces muscle tightness and spasms
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Decreases inflammation naturally
  • Safe for long-term use (when supervised)
  • Drug-free pain management option
  • Helps chronic pain patients live pain-free

How TENS Therapy Works

Electrical pulses delivered through electrodes:

Who Should Avoid TENS?

  • Pacemaker or cardiac device users
  • Pregnant women (consult physiotherapist)
  • Severe cardiac conditions
  • Open wounds or skin infection on treatment area
