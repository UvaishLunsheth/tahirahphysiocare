Ultrasound Therapy

Deep-heating physiotherapy modality that reduces inflammation, speeds tissue healing, and relieves pain using safe sound-wave technology.

Book Ultrasound Session

What is Ultrasound Therapy?

Ultrasound therapy uses high-frequency sound waves to penetrate deep into muscles, tendons and ligaments. The vibration produces gentle heat, which increases blood flow, accelerates healing, and reduces inflammation. It is one of the most effective physiotherapy modalities for soft-tissue injuries.

Benefits of Ultrasound Therapy

  • Reduces muscle and joint pain
  • Accelerates healing of tendons & ligaments
  • Breaks down scar tissue & adhesions
  • Reduces inflammation & swelling
  • Improves tissue flexibility & mobility
  • Enhances blood circulation to injured areas
  • Promotes safe and fast recovery

Conditions Treated with Ultrasound

  • Tendon injuries (tendinitis, tendinosis)
  • Muscle strain or spasm
  • Ligament sprains
  • Frozen shoulder
  • Plantar fasciitis
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Sports injuries
  • Post-operative stiffness
  • Scar tissue adhesions

How Ultrasound Therapy Works

The ultrasound probe sends sound waves that vibrate soft tissues at a microscopic level. This micro-vibration increases cellular activity, improves circulation, and promotes faster tissue repair. Our physiotherapists adjust the depth, frequency and intensity based on your condition.

Why Choose Tahirah PhysioCare?
