What is Cupping Therapy?

Cupping Therapy is a traditional pain-relief technique that uses vacuum suction to lift the skin and fascia. This stimulates blood flow, reduces muscle tightness, detoxifies tissues, and speeds up healing.

It is widely used by athletes, physiotherapists, and rehabilitation experts around the world.

Conditions Treated With Cupping Therapy

  • Back and neck pain
  • Muscle stiffness & tightness
  • Shoulder pain & frozen shoulder
  • Sports injuries
  • Myofascial pain syndrome
  • Headache due to muscle tension
  • Poor blood circulation
  • Detoxification & relaxation

Types of Cupping We Provide

  • Dry cupping
  • Static cupping
  • Dynamic / sliding cupping
  • Trigger point cupping

Benefits of Cupping Therapy

  • Reduces chronic pain & muscle stiffness
  • Improves circulation & oxygen supply
  • Speeds up muscle recovery
  • Detoxifies skin & deep tissues
  • Relieves stress & fatigue
  • Enhances mobility and flexibility
