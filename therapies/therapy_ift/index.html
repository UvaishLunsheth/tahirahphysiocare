What is Interferential Therapy (IFT)?

IFT is an advanced electrotherapy technique used in physiotherapy to relieve deep muscle pain, nerve pain and inflammation. It works by sending medium-frequency electrical currents that penetrate deeper tissues, providing long-lasting pain relief.

Benefits of IFT Therapy

  • Reduces pain quickly
  • Decreases swelling and inflammation
  • Releases muscle tightness
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Accelerates tissue healing
  • Reduces nerve pain (neuropathic pain)
  • Helps increase range of motion

Conditions We Treat with IFT

  • Back pain & lower back stiffness
  • Sciatica & nerve compression
  • Shoulder impingement / frozen shoulder
  • Knee pain (arthritis, ligament strain)
  • Neck pain & cervical spondylosis
  • Sports injuries
  • Muscle spasms & tightness
  • Post-operative or post-immobilization pain

How IFT Works

Two medium-frequency currents intersect inside the tissue, creating a therapeutic low-frequency effect deep inside muscles and joints. This helps reduce inflammation and blocks pain signals, offering rapid relief.
