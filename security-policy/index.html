Security Policy

At Tahirah PhysioCare, protecting patient information and clinic data is a top priority. We welcome responsible disclosure of any security vulnerabilities found on our website or digital systems.

1. Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a vulnerability, please report it responsibly via:

  • Email: info@tahirahphysiotherapy.com
  • Phone: +91 78747 39574

We request that you do not publicly disclose the issue until we confirm that it has been fixed.

2. What to Include in Your Report

Please share the following information (if available):

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • URL or affected endpoint
  • Proof-of-concept screenshots or video
  • Estimated impact

3. What We Do Not Permit

To protect our patients and systems, the following activities are prohibited:

  • Accessing patient records or attempting to bypass authentication
  • Performing DDoS or load-testing without permission
  • Sending malicious scripts or payloads
  • Social engineering, phishing, or impersonation of staff
