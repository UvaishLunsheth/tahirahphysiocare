Why fracture rehab is essential

After a fracture, joints become stiff and muscles weaken due to immobilization. Without rehab, long-term problems like pain, swelling and reduced mobility can persist. Physiotherapy accelerates safe recovery and restores full function.

Types of fractures we treat

  • Wrist & forearm fractures
  • Clavicle & shoulder fractures
  • Elbow & arm fractures
  • Ankle & foot fractures
  • Tibia / fibula fractures
  • Hip fractures
  • Post rod / plate / screw fixation

Treatment includes

  • Joint mobilization & stiffness reduction
  • Progressive strengthening exercises
  • Pain & swelling management
  • Balance & gait training
  • Weight-bearing progression
  • Home exercise program
