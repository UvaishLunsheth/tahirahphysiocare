Why choose our back pain physiotherapy?

At Tahirah PhysioCare, we focus on identifying the root cause of your pain and building a personalised plan to restore function. Our approach combines hands-on therapy, exercise progression, pain-relief modalities and lifestyle guidance so you get faster and lasting results.

Conditions we treat

  • Lower back pain & stiffness
  • Sciatica / nerve-related leg pain
  • Disc bulge / herniation
  • Muscle spasm & trigger points
  • Postural & occupational back pain
  • Spondylosis / degenerative spine conditions

Treatment techniques

  • Manual therapy & joint mobilization
  • Spinal stabilization & core strengthening

  • Dry needling / myofascial release (if indicated)
