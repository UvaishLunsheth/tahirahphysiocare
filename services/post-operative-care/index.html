Who needs post-operative rehab?

After surgery, the body requires structured physiotherapy to rebuild strength, restore mobility, reduce stiffness and ensure proper healing. We follow evidence-based protocols to guide you through each stage safely.

Surgeries we treat

  • Knee Replacement (TKR)
  • ACL / PCL ligament reconstruction
  • Fracture fixation (plates, rods, screws)
  • Spine surgery (discectomy, fusion)
  • Rotator cuff / shoulder surgeries
  • Hip replacement
  • Arthroscopy procedures

Treatment focus

  • Pain & swelling control
  • Range-of-motion restoration
  • Strengthening operated limb
  • Balance & gait training
  • Stiffness reduction
  • Functional training
  • Home exercise guidance
